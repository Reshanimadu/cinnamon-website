About Us

Joint Agri Products Ceylon (Pvt) Ltd, commonly known as JAPC has more than 03 decades worth of experience in the spice sector and currently operate as one of the major organic exporters in Sri Lanka’s industry. Among a number of product ranges, spices is the core product with main certifications like FairTrade and specially highest organic standards like Naturland, Demeter and Bio Suisse. Joint Agri operates as the largest company in exporting organic/pure/true cinnamon and a leader in exporting nutmegs and mace with guaranteed levels of aflatoxin and ochratoxin. JAPC main spice varieties being organic and FairTrade certified Ceylon cinnamon, Black pepper, White pepper, dehydrated Green pepper, nutmeg, mace, cloves, clove stems, lemon grass, curry leaves, vanilla, turmeric, ginger and moringa. We are also diversified in the product ranges offering Sri Lankan products such as organic coconut products, organic dehydrated fruits, organic essential oils and organic teas

Our production procedures use sophisticated and standardized methodologies which is backed up by the highest food safety certification of FSSC 22000. Each production step is closely monitored by highly skilled industry experts from the time of harvesting the raw materials up to dispatching the final product. JAPC is equipped with a 10,000 sq. Ft. modern spice manufacturing facility located at Weliweriya which is FSSC 22,000 certified/GFSI recognized since 2015. Joint Agri is among the very first industries to get a spice manufacturing plant FSSC 22000 certified in the country. Furthermore, we are proud to be one of the few companies in the world to have the highest certifications like Demeter, Naturland, Bio Suisse, Star-K Kosher, Halal, JAS, EU organic, and NOP/USDA with the products certified as FLO Fairtrade and Fairtrade USA. Not only that but JAPC owns its very own dehydrated fruit unit which caters to the dehydrated organic banana, papaya, mango, jack fruit, and pineapple products. Also, Joint Agri is the owner of an essential oil unit that produces lemon grass, nutmeg, and mace. Pepper, cinnamon, and clove essential oil items. When it comes to coconut products, we are maintaining long-term and strategic collaborations with BRC-certified manufacturers in order to fulfill the market demand for the highest quality standard products. In addition to these facilities, JAPC’s sister company which is located in up country, is catering to the company’s tea requirement.
