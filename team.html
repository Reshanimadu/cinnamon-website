Ceylon Cinnamon

Cinnamon Oils

The usage of cinnamon can be tracked up to ancient Egyptian times which is considered as valuable than gold. Its sweet smell is considered a sign of fortune, health, and security. The cinnamon essential oil is highly sought after in aromatherapy which has a precious load of health properties.

The chemical composition of both cinnamon essential oil and Ceylon cinnamon essential oil are alike due to the presence of chemicals such as eugenol and cinnamaldehyde but the sourcing is different. The traditional cinnamon is produced from the Cinnamomum cassia tree and the Ceylon cinnamon oil is extracted from the Cinnamomum verum variety.

It has a combination of sweet, spicy, and woody smells with a long-lasting aroma. Due to it’s warm and comforting smell, this is has a high demand in the aromatherapy. Even though it is generally considered as safe to use, it’s better to avoid high concentration levels due to the high potency nature of the oil. This oil is produced from the outer bark or leaves of the Cinnamomum tree and the bark oil is higher in demand since it has a more potent spiciness.
